Norwich own authenctic Brazillian Street food

Sabor Dos Santos

"Saintly flavours"

Bringing the vibrant taste of Brazil to the heart of Norwich, our street food stall is all about bold flavours, sizzling aromas, and authentic recipes made with love. From golden coxinhas and crispy pastéis to mouthwatering grilled meats and sweet treats, we serve up the best of Brazilian street food – fresh, fast, and full of soul. Whether you’re looking for a quick bite on the go or a flavour-packed meal to share with friends, our stall is a little slice of Brazil right here in the UK. Come for the food, stay for the atmosphere – and let every bite transport you to the bustling streets of Rio, São Paulo, and beyond.
